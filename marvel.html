The Original Avengers

Iron Man

Thor

Thor is the God of Thunder, wielding the mighty hammer Mjolnir.

Hulk

Bruce Banner transforms into the Hulk, a giant green powerhouse.

Black Widow

Natasha Romanoff is Black Widow, a master spy and assassin.

Hawkeye
